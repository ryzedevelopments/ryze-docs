# Hostage Rules

## 1.5 — Hostage Engagement Rules

You may not ask someone to take you hostage.

Taking a police officer hostage for banks is allowed only when 6+ police are in the city.

Taking a police officer hostage to recover seized items is allowed within 30 minutes of the incident.

You may not take a police officer hostage to break someone out of prison.

Maximum hostages: 4

One demand per hostage.

All demands must be clear, reasonable, and achievable.
