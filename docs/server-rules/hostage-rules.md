# 🪢 1.5 Hostage Rules

## Hostage Engagement Rules

- 1.5.1 You may not ask someone to take you hostage
- 1.5.2 Taking a police officer hostage for banks is allowed only when **6+ police** are in the city
- 1.5.3 Taking a police officer hostage to recover seized items is allowed **within 30 minutes** of the incident
- 1.5.4 You may not take a police officer hostage to break someone out of prison
- 1.5.5 Maximum hostages: **4**
- 1.5.6 One demand per hostage
- 1.5.7 All demands must be clear, reasonable, and achievable
