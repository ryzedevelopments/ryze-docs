# 💬 Character & Communication

## 1.3 — Communication & Conduct Standards

### All communication must remain respectful.

### English is required for all IC and OOC communication.

### No Metagaming:

OOC information may not be used for IC advantage.

### A clear, working microphone is required.

### No unrealistic or comedic voice changers for IC use.

### No abusive, toxic, or hostile communication.

### Stay in character and maintain appropriate language at all times.

### No Multi-Character Cross-Benefit:

Alternate characters may not assist or benefit your main character.
