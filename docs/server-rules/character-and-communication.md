# 💬 1.3 Character & Communication

## Communication & Conduct Standards

- 1.3.1 All communication must remain respectful
- 1.3.2 English is required for all IC and OOC communication
- 1.3.3 A clear, working microphone is required
- 1.3.4 No unrealistic or comedic voice changers for IC use
- 1.3.5 No abusive, toxic, or hostile communication
- 1.3.6 Stay in character and maintain appropriate language at all times

---

**1.3.7 No Metagaming**
OOC information may not be used for IC advantage.

**1.3.8 No Multi-Character Cross-Benefit**
Alternate characters may not assist or benefit your main character.
