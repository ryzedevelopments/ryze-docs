# 💸 2.2 Robbery Rules

## Criminal Engagement & Theft Protocols

Robberies must be story-driven, realistic, and respectful of server standards. Random, low-effort, or predatory robberies will result in disciplinary action.

---

## Robbery Eligibility Rules

**2.2.1 EMS Protection**
Medical personnel may never be robbed.

**2.2.2 Police Robberies**
Police may only be robbed during a valid RP scenario with sufficient police presence. Government-issued items may not be taken.

**2.2.3 Life Preservation**
Victims must value their life — no radio use, weapon pulling, or escalation.

**2.2.4 Targeting Rules**
Repeatedly robbing the same players is prohibited.

**2.2.5 Safe Zone Protection**
No robberies in green zones or legal crafting areas.

**2.2.6 RP Justification**
Robberies must have a valid RP reason — not random or senseless.

**2.2.7 Recording Requirement**
All robberies must be recorded from start to finish.

**2.2.8 Legal Items**
Clean money, phones, bandages, and other legal items may not be stolen.

---

## 2.2.9 Two-Minute Interaction Standard

Before initiating a robbery:

- Minimum **2 minutes** of meaningful RP
- Must include legitimate build-up to hostility
- Random small talk or filler questions do not count

:::info
Robberies must feel like RP, not a mechanical transaction.
:::
