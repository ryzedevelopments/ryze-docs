# Evidence & Reporting

## 1.8 — 🎥 Recording, Evidence & Reporting Protocol

Proper evidence and respectful communication ensure fair outcomes for all players.

## Recording Requirements

Recordings must show the entire interaction, start to finish.

Reports without clips/IDs will not proceed unless logs confirm the incident.

Use F10 to obtain Player IDs.

## Reporting Conduct

Do not tag or @ staff.

Do not spam reports or tags.

All staff interactions must remain respectful.

Arguing with staff in tickets or in-city may result in escalation.

If needed, another staff member will review the situation.

## Ryze RP Enforcement Standard

Ryze RP enforces:

Realistic vehicle behavior

Respect for safe zones

Proper evidence handling

Professional communication with staff

Players who cannot uphold these standards will be removed from the community.
