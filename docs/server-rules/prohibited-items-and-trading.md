# 🚫 2.1 Prohibited Items & Trading

---

## Restricted Weapons & Premium Vehicles

These items are protected to maintain server balance and fairness.

### 2.1.1 Unbreakable Weapons

:::danger

- Stealing unbreakable or government-issued weapons is prohibited
- Selling, trading, gifting, or transferring unbreakable weapons is not allowed under any circumstances
- These items are non-circulating and may not enter the player economy
  :::

### 2.1.2 Premium / Coin Vehicles

:::danger

- Selling coin vehicles for in-city money, items, or services is prohibited
- Trading coin vehicles between players is not allowed
- Scamming involving coin vehicles will result in severe punishment, including potential permanent bans
- Coin vehicles are non-transferable and may not be used for economic gain
  :::

---

## 2.2 — IRL Trading & Out-of-City Transactions

Ryze RP enforces a **zero-tolerance policy** on real-world trading.

**2.2.1 Attempted IRL Trading**
Attempting to negotiate, advertise, or arrange IRL trades — even if the trade never occurs — is a rule breach.

**2.2.2 IRL Trading**
Buying, selling, or trading any in-game items, vehicles, money, or services for real-world currency (PayPal, bank transfer, PayID, crypto, gift cards, etc.) is strictly prohibited.

**Punishment**
:::danger
Permanent ban with a full wipe.
:::
