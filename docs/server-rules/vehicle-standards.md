# 🚗 Vehicle Standards

Vehicle Operation & Pursuit Standards

Vehicle usage must remain realistic, grounded, and consistent with GTA V physics. Abuse of vehicle mechanics will result in disciplinary action.

## Driving Expectations

Driving must follow realistic GTA V standards.

Small jumps are acceptable; stunt-driving is not.

No excessive ramming of civilians or police.

Civilians may not ram police to force or start a pursuit.

Emergency vehicles (EMS / Fire / PD) may never be stolen.

## Passenger & Carry Rules

Bikes may carry a maximum of two riders.

Drivers may not carry players; passengers may.

## Life Preservation in Vehicles

If a firearm is aimed at you while seated in a vehicle, you must fear for your life.

Melee weapons do not trigger forced fear compliance.

## Boot & Vehicle Searches

A vehicle must be out for 10+ minutes before a boot search is valid.

Boot checks are not allowed in green zones or garages.

## Prohibited Actions

Do not drive over downed players to avoid arrest.

No unrealistic stunt chains, wall rides, or physics abuse.
