# 🛡️ 2.3 Final Conduct

## Ryze RP — Final Conduct Rules

These rules exist to protect the integrity of RP, prevent OOC disrespect, and keep all interactions grounded in character.

:::warning
Any breach of these standards will result in immediate disciplinary action.
:::

---

## 2.3.1 In-Character Trash Talk

Trash talk is allowed only when it fits the RP and makes sense within the scenario.

- Let tension build naturally during conflicts
- Keep all insults in-character
- Do not force disrespect for no RP reason

:::danger
Anything that becomes OOC, personal, or real-life related is strictly prohibited.
:::

Ryze RP is a character-driven environment — not a place for real-life hostility.

---

## 2.3.2 Strictly Forbidden Actions

The following actions are never allowed, under any circumstances:

:::danger

- Sexual emotes or explicit actions directed at other players
- Pissing, shitting, or degrading bodily-function emotes
- Spitting, `/me` trolling, or humiliation RP without consent
- Racist, homophobic, or discriminatory language — even "in-character"
- Targeted harassment through text, voice, or emotes
- **VDM** — intentionally running someone over is always prohibited
  :::

These behaviors break immersion, violate community standards, and will be met with immediate punishment.

---

## Ryze RP Conduct Standard

Players are expected to maintain:

- Respect for others
- Clear separation between IC and OOC
- Realistic, grounded RP
- Zero tolerance for harassment or shock-value behavior

Ryze RP is built on quality, character-driven storytelling — not shock RP or personal attacks.
