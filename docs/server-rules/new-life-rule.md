# 🧠 New Life Rule

## 1.2 — New Life Protocol

Upon death, you forget how, why, and where the incident occurred.

You may not seek revenge on anyone involved in your death.

You may only retain information if a surviving member witnessed the event and relays it to you.

If revived by EMS, the New Life Protocol does not apply.
