# 🧠 1.2 New Life Rule

## New Life Protocol

- 1.2.1 Upon death, you forget how, why, and where the incident occurred
- 1.2.2 You may not seek revenge on anyone involved in your death
- 1.2.3 You may only retain information if a surviving member witnessed the event and relays it to you

:::info
1.2.4 If revived by EMS, the New Life Protocol does not apply.
:::
