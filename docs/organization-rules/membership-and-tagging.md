# Membership & Tagging

Ryze RP — Untagged Members

To keep the ORG ecosystem fair, balanced, and immersive within Ryze RP, every group must operate under a clear structure. These rules ensure that leadership and affiliates follow the same expectations and maintain proper roleplay quality.

## Untagged Members

Untagged members are individuals who associate with a crew but are not officially registered on the roster.

### They may:

Take part in non-hostile RP with the org

Act as Scouts during hostility (maximum of 1 allowed)

### They may NOT:

Participate in any hostile RP with the org

Untagged members WILL be placed on a 2 week cooldown if caught breaking this rule.

---

Ryze RP — Tagged Members

Tagged members are official, fully recognized members who carry the responsibilities and privileges of representing their org in all situations. Once tagged, a person is considered an active part of the orgs identity, reputation, and actions.

## Tagged Members

Tagged members have full access to crew operations and may:

Represent the crew in hostile, defensive, or high-stakes RP

Use crew resources, including stash items, turf systems, and spray mechanics

Participate in wars, raids, territory conflicts, and official crew events

## Tagging Requirements

To ensure fairness and prevent abuse, the following rules apply:

A newly tagged member cannot engage in hostile RP for 24 hours

(This cooldown prevents instant tagging for combat advantage)

A player must have a minimum of 2 days of playtime before they are eligible to be tagged

## Leadership Responsibilities

Crew leaders are responsible for:

Ensuring every tagged member understands the rules, expectations, and consequences of representing the crew

Maintaining roster integrity and preventing misuse of tagging for combat stacking

Upholding Ryze RP’s standards for quality, story-driven roleplay
