# 📢 Reporting & Witch Hunting

## Reporting Expectations

All reports involving organizations must be supported with appropriate evidence.

Clips should clearly show the full interaction and alleged breach.

Staff reserve the right to request additional evidence where necessary.

## Witch Hunting

Attempting to rally other players against an organization outside of roleplay is prohibited.

Repeated targeting of players or organizations through OOC means is considered witch hunting.

Organizing harassment campaigns, encouraging reports without cause, or spreading misinformation to damage another group's reputation may result in disciplinary action.

All disputes should be handled through proper reporting channels.

Organizations are expected to maintain professionalism both in and out of character.
