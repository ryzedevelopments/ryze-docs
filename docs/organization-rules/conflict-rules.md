# ⚡O.8 Conflict Rules

## Ryze RP — Hostility & Conflict Rules

Hostility in Ryze RP must stay controlled, fair, and immersive.

All organizations are expected to follow these rules to prevent chaos, third-partying, and unrealistic war stacking.

---

## O.8.1 1v1 Conflict Rule

Hostile engagements are strictly one organization versus one organization.

If Org A is in an active conflict with Org B, no other crew may:

- Initiate new beef
- Interfere in ongoing fights
- Declare a separate war on either side

No third-partying under any circumstances.

:::danger
Any attempt to insert a third party into an active conflict will result in immediate action from Staff.
:::

---

## O.8.2 48-Hour Cooling-Off Period

When a war or major conflict ends, all involved organizations enter a mandatory 48-hour peace period.

### During this cooldown:

**O.8.2.1 No Re-Engagement**
You may not attack the same organization for 48 hours.

**O.8.2.2 No Chain-Warring**
You may not start a new conflict with any other organization until the cooldown expires.

**O.8.2.3 Passive RP Only**
Crews should focus on:

- Restocking
- Healing and aftermath RP
- Internal development
- Non-violent storylines

### O.8.2.4 Baiting During Cooldown

:::warning
If an organization is seen baiting, provoking, or attempting to force hostile RP during cooldown without valid RP reasoning, Staff will take action against the individuals involved.

Further consequences may follow depending on severity.
:::

---

## O.8.3 Expectations of Conduct

All organizations must approach conflict with:

- Realistic escalation
- Clear RP intent
- Respect for server balance
- Zero exploitation of loopholes

Ryze RP prioritizes quality over chaos. Crews that cannot maintain discipline will face penalties.
