# 🏠 Compound Rules

Ryze RP — Compound Breaching Rules

Compound safety is a privilege, not a shield to exploit.

Breaching is only allowed when an organization forfeits its protection through clear hostile actions.

## Aggression Triggers

A compound cannot be breached unless one of the following triggers occurs:

### Hostile Fire From Inside

If an organization fires shots from within their compound at players outside, they immediately lose compound protection.

The defending party may breach and neutralize the threat.

This includes warning shots, potshots, or any intentional discharge toward another group.

### Active Baiting Behind Gates

If a crew uses their compound as a shield while taunting, provoking, recording rivals, or baiting conflict, they create a Fair Shooting Zone.

Baiting removes compound immunity.

Staff will treat this as intentional escalation.

### Hot Pursuit

If a gunfight begins outside and one side retreats into their compound to avoid consequences, the opposing party may continue the engagement inside.

Running into a gate mid-fight does not reset the scenario.

You cannot use your compound as a “safe room” during active combat.

## General Conduct Expectations

### Value Your Life

Just because breaching becomes allowed does not mean it becomes smart.

Organizations must still consider:

Numbers

Positioning

Realistic survival

Whether the breach makes sense in RP

### No Compound Camping

Sitting inside your compound all day waiting for someone to breach is considered Fail RP.

This includes constant gate-peeking, baiting, or hiding behind doors to force engagements.

### Record the Trigger

Clipping the initial provocation is strongly recommended.

Shots fired

Baiting

Retreating mid-fight

This protects your organization in case of a report.

## Server Standard

Ryze RP expects all organizations to treat compounds as RP spaces, not loopholes.

Any attempt to weaponize compound mechanics will result in Staff action.
