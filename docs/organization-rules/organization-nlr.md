# ☠️ O.10 Organization NLR

## NLR / KIA Protocols

---

## O.10.1 What NLR Means

When your character bleeds out or dies they lose all memory of the situation that killed them.

Death is a hard reset, not a shortcut back into the fight.

---

## O.10.2 The 30-Minute NLR Timer

Once you respawn, you enter a 30-minute NLR cooldown.

### ✔️ During NLR You MUST:

- Stay away from the area where you died

### ❌ You CANNOT:

- Return to the scene
- Lurk nearby
- Re-enter the conflict in any way

:::danger
Breaking NLR will result in heavy fines and potential staff action.
:::

:::info
The default 30 minutes may change if both sides agree to different terms during KOS negotiations.
:::

---

## O.10.3 Removing Gang Identity (Mandatory)

The moment you respawn, you must strip all gang identifiers:

- No vests
- No colours
- No patches
- No gang-specific clothing

:::warning
You cannot represent your org while under NLR.
:::

---

## O.10.4 KIA LOG SYSTEM (Mandatory for KOS & Beef)

### O.10.4.1 When to Post KIA

If you die during:

- A KOS, or
- Any active beef/conflict _(NOT official war)_

You must post **"KIA"** in the designated Discord channel the moment you hit the floor.

This keeps combat trackable and prevents confusion.
