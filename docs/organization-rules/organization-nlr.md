# Organization NLR

NLR / KIA Protocols

## 🟥 What NLR Means

When your character bleeds out or dies they lose all memory of the situation that killed them.

Death is a hard reset, not a shortcut back into the fight.

NLR Meaning — Your character cannot remember anything leading to their death.

## 🟥 The 30-Minute NLR Timer

Once you respawn, you enter a 30-minute NLR cooldown.

### ✔️ During NLR You MUST:

Stay away from the area where you died

### ❌ You CANNOT:

Return to the scene

Lurk nearby

Re-enter the conflict in any way

Breaking NLR will result in heavy fines and potential staff action.

Note: The default 30 minutes may change if both sides agree to different terms during KOS negotiations.

NLR Timer Rules — You must fully reset after death.

## 🟥 Removing Gang Identity (Mandatory)

The moment you respawn, you must strip all gang identifiers:

No vests

No colours

No patches

No gang-specific clothing

Gang Clothing Reset — You cannot represent your org while under NLR.

## 📢・KIA LOG SYSTEM (Mandatory for KOS & Beef)

### 🟥 When to Post KIA

If you die during:

A KOS, or

Any active beef/conflict (NOT official war)

You must post "KIA" in the designated Discord channel the moment you hit the floor.

This keeps combat trackable and prevents confusion.
