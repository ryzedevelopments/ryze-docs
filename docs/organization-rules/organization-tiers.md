# 📈 O.3 Organization Tiers

## Ryze RP — ORG Structure & Clothing Progression

In Ryze RP, ranking up is earned through consistency, presence, and real RP impact.

Every tier unlocks more numbers, more customization, and more responsibility.

Gang Staff judge progression on long-term performance, not short bursts of activity.

---

## O.3.1 Tier 1 — Ghost Tier

> Your first footprint in the streets — nobody knows you yet, but the city is watching.

| Property        | Value      |
| --------------- | ---------- |
| Roster Cap      | 20 members |
| Hostile Cap     | 15 members |
| Custom Clothing | None       |

You're unproven — this tier is about showing you even belong in the conversation.

---

## O.3.2 Tier 2 — Street Tier

> Your name's starting to circulate — people recognize your colors and movement.

| Property        | Value      |
| --------------- | ---------- |
| Roster Cap      | 25 members |
| Hostile Cap     | 20 members |
| Custom Clothing | 5 pieces   |

You're gaining traction — now you must prove you can operate with consistency.

---

## O.3.3 Tier 3 — Trial Tier

> This is the proving ground — show you're different, show you're built for the top.

| Property        | Value       |
| --------------- | ----------- |
| Roster Cap      | 30 members  |
| Hostile Cap     | 25 members  |
| Custom Clothing | 8 pieces    |
| Unlocks         | Gun Vendors |

This tier tests your structure — only disciplined crews make it past here.

---

## O.3.4 Tier 4 — Verified Tier

> You've built structure, claimed turf, and earned real presence in the city.

| Property        | Value                                             |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Roster Cap      | 35 members                                        |
| Hostile Cap     | 30 members                                        |
| Custom Clothing | 10 pieces                                         |
| Unlocks         | Custom MLO (must be provided), Drug & Gun Vendors |

You're now an established power — a crew with influence, identity, and proven dominance.
