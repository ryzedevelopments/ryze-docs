# 💵 Demands & Payouts

## ⚠️ Fear RP Requirement

If the hostage's crew refuses to meet reasonable demands and shows no fear for their member's life, that member may be Hard-PK'd from the org.

This is considered RP blocking and will be treated seriously.

Hostage Rules — Hostage situations must be taken seriously.

## 🟥 Financial Demand Limits

To keep payouts balanced, the following maximum caps apply depending on the rank of the person involved:

### Leadership (Top 3)

Max: $15,000,000

### Lower Hierarchy

Max: $7,000,000

### Members

Max: $5,000,000

### Prospects

Max: $1,000,000

These caps apply to Beef and KOS-based conflicts, not official wars unless agreed otherwise.

Financial Caps — Payouts scale with rank.

## 🟥 Weapon Demand Limits

Weapon demands must stay within realistic limits.

### MAX GUN DEMANDS

10 craftable guns total

### Two-Hander Limit

No more than 5 two-handers

Remaining guns must be SMGs or Shotguns

### Unbreakables

Unbreakable weapons cannot be demanded.

Weapon Limits — Weapon payouts must stay balanced.

## 🟥 Stolen Clothing Demands

If you steal another org's clothing (vest, kutte, jacket, etc.), you may demand payment for its return.

### Pricing Caps

Top 3: $10,000,000

Lower Hierarchy: $5,000,000

Member: $2,000,000

Prospect: $100,000

### Proof Requirement

You must provide:

A clip showing you stealing the clothing, AND

The owner visible in the clip

No clip = no valid demand.

Clothing Demands — Clothing payouts require proof.

## 🟥 2. GOVERNMENT APPROVAL PROCESS (WAR ONLY)

Official War Terms must be reviewed by Government to ensure fairness and prevent abuse.

### Step 1 — Post the Terms

In the War Declaration Channel, you must post:

Full war terms

All demands

All conditions

Any tactical rules

### Step 2 — Gov Review

A Government member will:

React with a ✅, or

Send a message confirming approval

If changes are needed, Gov will request them.

### Step 3 — War Becomes Official

Once Gov approves:

The war is official

KOS/Conflict may begin under the approved terms

Gov Approval — War cannot start without Gov greenlight.
