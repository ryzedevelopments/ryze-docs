# 🚨 O.12 Strike System

## Ryze RP — Strike System

The Strike System exists to hold crews accountable through immersive, in-city consequences, not instant removals. Strikes scale based on severity, and punishments are enforced in character whenever possible. Staff only steps in directly when a situation requires it.

Strikes impact a crew's money, time, reputation, and progression in the city.

---

## O.12.1 Minor Strikes

Minor Strikes are issued for low-level issues that harm the RP environment or show poor crew conduct.

### Examples include:

- Low-effort or lack of meaningful roleplay
- Poor behaviour during gang conflicts
- Disrespectful or negative interactions with PD or civilians
- Any behaviour that damages immersion or breaks server rules

Minor Strikes are issued at staff discretion — this list does not cover every scenario.

### O.12.1.1 Minor Strike Fees

Each Minor Strike adds an in-city financial penalty:

| Strike           | Fee        |
| ---------------- | ---------- |
| 1st Minor Strike | $3,000,000 |
| 2nd Minor Strike | $5,000,000 |
| 3rd Minor Strike | $8,000,000 |

These fees must be paid in character and represent the crew's loss of stability, resources, and influence.

---

## O.12.2 Major Strikes

Major Strikes are issued for serious rule violations, regardless of how many Minor Strikes a crew has.
