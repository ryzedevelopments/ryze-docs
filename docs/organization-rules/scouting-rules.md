# Scouting Rules

## Scout Standards

Scouts exist to gather information and support roleplay progression, not to artificially inflate numbers during hostilities.

### Untagged Scouts

Untagged associates may act as Scouts during hostility.

Maximum of 1 Scout allowed.

Scouts may observe and relay information.

Scouts may NOT:

Participate in shootouts

Hold hostages

Provide direct combat assistance

Carry out hostile actions

### Tagged Members Acting as Scouts

Tagged members acting in a scouting capacity remain bound by all hostile RP rules.

Scouting should enhance roleplay and intelligence gathering.

It must never be used to circumvent hostile caps or create unfair advantages.

Any attempt to abuse scout mechanics for combat stacking may result in staff action.
