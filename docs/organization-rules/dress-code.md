# 👕 Dress Code

## Organization Clothing Standards

Organization clothing represents identity, reputation, and progression. It must be respected and used appropriately.

Organizations may only use the amount of custom clothing allocated to their current tier.

Custom clothing must be approved through the appropriate channels before use.

Organizations may not use clothing that:

Impersonates emergency services

Impersonates Government officials

Impersonates another organization

Uses copyrighted or inappropriate imagery

All clothing should align with the organization's theme and identity.

## Clothing Restrictions

Organizations may not copy or closely imitate another organization's established look.

Any attempt to deliberately confuse identity through clothing similarities may result in staff intervention.

Clothing Restrictions — You cannot impersonate another org.
